About the Lion

The lion, often called the "King of the Jungle," is a large feline native to Africa and parts of India. Lions live in social groups called prides, consisting of multiple females, their cubs, and a dominant male.

They are known for their powerful roars, which can be heard up to 5 miles away. Lions are apex predators, meaning they are at the top of the food chain.

Interesting Facts:

  • Male lions have majestic manes that grow darker with age.
  • They can run at speeds of up to 50 mph for short distances.
  • Unlike most cats, lions are very social animals.
